Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request a barangay document?
Go to the Barangay Services page, select the service you need, fill up the form, and follow the steps to submit your requirements and payment.
How can I track my request status?
Use the Track Request page and enter your name or reference number to view the status of your request.
What documents do I need to upload?
The required documents depend on the service. The requirements will be shown after you select a service.
How do I contact support?
You can use the contact form below to send us your questions or concerns.
